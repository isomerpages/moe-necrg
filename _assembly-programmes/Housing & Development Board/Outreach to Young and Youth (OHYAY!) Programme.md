<p>HDB’s OHYAY! programme seeks to raise awareness on good neighbourly messages
and to nurture students in building active and caring communities in the
heartlands. Students will attend an interactive physical roadshow and will
be encouraged to complete an e-learning module as part of post-roadshow
engagement which helps reinforce the key messages learnt.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>The outreach consists of an interactive physcial roadshow and an e-learning
module. The physical roadshow engages students through an audio-visual
presentation, liv interactions, and QnA sessions while the e-learning module
reinforces the values learnt through infographic sharing and role play.</p>
<p><strong>Learning Objectives</strong>
</p>
<p>Primary school students will learn and pick up responsible neighbourly
behaviour and practical eco-friendly practices. Secondary school students
will learn how the design of public housing facilitates the building of
communities and how they can contribute towards promoting active and caring
community bonds.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience</strong>: Primary 1 - 6 students, Secondary 1
- 4/5 students</p>
<p><strong>Runtime</strong>: All year round</p>
<p><strong>Fee</strong>: Complimentary</p>
<p><strong>Modality</strong>: Physical (Note: The programme will be held
physically from 1 April 2026).</p>
<p><strong>Duration of Session</strong>: Physical Roadshow: 45 minutes; E-Learning
Module: 20 minutes</p>
<p><strong>Relevant Commemorative Days</strong>: Total Defence Day, International
Friendship Day, Racial Harmony Day</p>
<p><strong>Website</strong>: Go.gov.sg/hdb-ohyay</p>
<p><strong>Mode of Registration:</strong>&nbsp;By email, online through website
link</p>
<p><strong>Due Date for registration</strong>: 3 weeks in advance</p>
<p><strong>Contact</strong>: HDB_Community_Partnerships@hdb.gov.sg</p>
