<h3>NParks: School Plays</h3>
<p>Discover Singapore's rich biodiversity and learn how each of us can play
a part in protecting the ecosystem and transforming Singapore into a City
in Nature through our school plays.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>Engaged by NParks, a drama company will stage an interactive school play
at the school during assembly period. Students can participate in the quizzes
during the play.</p>
<p><strong>Learning Objectives</strong>
</p>
<p>Students will learn about nature and the characteristics of various animals
that inhabit Singapore.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience</strong>: Primary 1 - 6 students</p>
<p><strong>Runtime:</strong>&nbsp;All year round</p>
<p><strong>Fee:</strong>&nbsp;Complimentary</p>
<p><strong>Modality:</strong>&nbsp;In-person</p>
<p><strong>Duration of Session:</strong>&nbsp;45 mins</p>
<p><strong>Capacity:</strong> 1000 pax</p>
<p><strong>Mode of Registration:</strong>&nbsp;By email - <a href="mailto:chelsey_yang@nparks.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">chelsey_yang@nparks.gov.sg</a>
</p>
<p><strong>Due Date for registration</strong>: Registration opens in August
each year</p>
