<h3>SKM: Primary Assembly Show</h3>
<p>SKMâ€™s assembly show in interactive drama theatre present on Racial Harmony
is a dynamic and thought-provoking assembly show that brings everyday scenarios
of bias, stereotypes and misunderstandings to life.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>The Primary School Assembly Show follows three children from different
racial backgrounds in Singapore who, through misunderstandings on Racial
Harmony Day, discover that their cultural differences connect rather than
divide them.&nbsp;</p>
<p>
<br><strong>Learning Objectives</strong>
</p>
<p>Students will:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Explore what racial harmony means in a multicultural society like Singapore</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reflect on how words and actions can affect others</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Recognise the impact of unconscious bias and exclusion</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Practise standing up and speaking out with kindness and respect</p>
</li>
</ul>
