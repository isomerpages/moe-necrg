# ABC WATERS LEARNING TRAIL
Under the Active, Beautiful, Clean Waters (ABC Waters) Programme, Singapore's network of drains, canals and reservoirs are transformed into beautiful and vibrant waterscapes for recreation, community bonding and outdoor learning.

**Details**

The ABC Waters Learning Trails is a series of place-based experiential trails that enables students to explore, discover and learn more about water is an engaging and hands-on manner at the ABC Waters sites.

**Learning Objectives**

Students will appreciate the importance of keeping our waterways and catchment areas clean, learn about water sustainability, Singapore's Four National Taps and design features of the ABC Water sites and understand the importance of taking stewardship of our waters.
