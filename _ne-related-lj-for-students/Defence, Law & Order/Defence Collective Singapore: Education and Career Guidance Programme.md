<p>In this Education and Career Guidance (ECG) experience, students will
step into the shoes of Career Detectives, Mission Planners, and Insight
Scouts to explore the many paths that keep Singapore safe and strong. The
session culminates in an inspirational sharing by a serving personnel or
veteran, offering authentic insights into career journeys, challenges,
and the sense of purpose behind defending our home.</p>
<p></p>

</p>

and uncover the skills that define key defence vocations. Additionally,
