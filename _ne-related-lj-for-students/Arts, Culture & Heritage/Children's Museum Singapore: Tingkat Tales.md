<h3>Children's Museum Singapore</h3>
<h4>Learning Journey: Tingkat Tales</h4>
<p>Embark on an interactive learning journey with the magical Tingkat to
discover stories of the past, present and future of hawker culture. Through
this learning journey, pupils will learn about the diverse food cultures
of Singapore and gain an appreciation for our unique food heritage.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>This programme has a 40 minute facilitated journey at level 2 with our
museum facilitators, along with a self-exploration of all the galleries.</p>
<p><strong>Learning Objectives:</strong> Students will find out more about
local dishes that became Singapore's signature dishes, street hawkers in
the past, and food cultures of the different communities in Singapore.
They will also learn to appreciate the uniqueness of hawker centres in
Singapore.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience: </strong>Pri 1 to 3 students</p>
<p><strong>Runtime:</strong> All year round</p>
<p><strong>Fee: </strong>$15 per pax</p>
<p><strong>Modality:</strong> In-person</p>
<p><strong>Duration of Session:</strong> 45 minutes</p>
<p><strong>Programme Capacity: </strong>80 per session</p>
<p><strong>Website:</strong> Visit <a href="http://bookmuseums.nhb.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">bookmuseums.nhb.gov.sg</a> to make
