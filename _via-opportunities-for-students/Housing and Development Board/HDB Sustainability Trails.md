<h4>HDB Sustainability Trails</h4>
<p>The HDB Sustainability Trails offer an immersive experience for students,
allowing them to discover the various green initiatives and sustainability
features aimed at promoting sustainable living in HDB heartlands. Under
the programme, students and teachers could also sign up to be an Eco Guide
and undergo training to lead the trails for their peers/ students and the
wider community.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>Students could participate in a 2-hour guided trails or sign up to be
an Eco Guide.</p>
<p>The training for Eco Guide includes a 2-hour classroom training, followed
by another 2 hours of on-site training, conducted on separate days. Upon
completing the training, students are empowered to organise trails for
their peers and/or the community.</p>
<p>The trails feature places such as:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>MyWaterway @ Punggol</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Treelodge @ Punggol</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Northshore Residences</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Northshore Plaza</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Learning Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<p>Students will gain a comprehensive understanding of the transformation
of Punggol and the efforts made to enable sustainable living, thereby inspiring
them to do their part for the environment and adopting a green lifestyle.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience: </strong>Pri 4 to 6, Sec 1 to 4/5, Post-Sec (JC/Poly/ITE)</p>
<p><strong>Runtime: </strong>All year round</p>
<p><strong>Fee: </strong>Complimentary</p>
<p><strong>Modality:</strong> In-person</p>
<p><strong>Duration of Session:</strong> Average 2 hours per session. Number
of sessions would vary, depending on preferred involvement (i.e. whether
students are signing up as Eco Guides)</p>
<p><strong>Programme Capacity: </strong>20 minimum pax per session</p>
<p><strong>Mode of Registration: </strong>By email</p>
<p><strong>Due Date for Registration: </strong>A lead time of 2 months is
required</p>
