<h3>Singapore Kindness Movement</h3>
<h4>Kindsville Times/A-OK! Newsletters &amp; Animations</h4>
<p>Kindsville Times and A-OK! are newsletters that introduce kindness topics
to primary school students. The newsletters are available in age-appropriate
versions: Kindsville Times Junior (Primary 1 and 2), Kindsville Times Adventures
(Primary 3 and 4) and A-OK! (Primary 5 and 6). Kindsville Times will be
accompanied by a 5-minute ‘Singa and the Kindness Cubbies’ animation episode,
launched online.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>Students can read and complete the newsletter activities in school or
at home. They can participate in a class discussion based on the activities
or contests. Additionally, students can complete activities together with
their family members in the “Family Time” section.</p>
<p><strong>Learning Objectives</strong>
</p>
<p>Through age-appropriate activities, comics, kindness tips and contests,
students will learn about the importance of specific character values and
positive attitudes towards school-life challenges.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience</strong>: Primary 1 - P6 students</p>
<p><strong>Runtime</strong>: January, April, July, August, October</p>
<p><strong>Fee</strong>: Complimentary</p>
<p><strong>Modality</strong>: In-person</p>
<p><strong>Relevant Commemorative Days</strong>: Total Defence Day, International
Friendship Day, Racial Harmony Day, National Day</p>

</p>
