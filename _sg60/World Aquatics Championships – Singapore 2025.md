<h3>World Aquatics Championships - Singapore 2025</h3>
<p><strong>Programme Details:</strong>
</p>
<p>World Aquatics Championships – Singapore 2025 is the flagship event bringing
together the six aquatic sports of swimming, water polo, diving, artistic
swimming, open water swimming and high diving. The premier event will take
place from 11 July to 3 August at the Singapore Sports Hub and Sentosa.
The Championships is expected to attract over 2,500 elite athletes from
over 200 national federations.</p>
