<h2>President's Volunteerism &amp; Philanthropy Awards</h2>
<p>The President’s Volunteerism &amp; Philanthropy Awards is the pinnacle
award and platform to recognise individuals, organisations and leaders
and cross-sector partnerships that have achieved excellence in giving.
These awards represent the highest honour for giving to the community and
are conferred by the President of the Republic of Singapore. It is organised
annually by the National Volunteer and Philanthropy Centre (NVPC), with
support from the Ministry of Culture, Community and Youth (MCCY).</p>
<p><strong>What this award means for schools:</strong>
</p>
<p>• Encouraging teachers to nominate and students to apply helps bring recognition
to young changemakers within your zone and the school community</p>
<p>• Highlights schools’ commitment to fostering values of volunteerism,
leadership, and social responsibility</p>
<p>• A student’s win motivates the school community others to contribute
positively to Singapore</p>
<p>• It reinforces schools’ role in shaping future leaders and strengthens
schools’ ties with the broader community</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience: </strong>Students (19 years old &amp; below)</p>
