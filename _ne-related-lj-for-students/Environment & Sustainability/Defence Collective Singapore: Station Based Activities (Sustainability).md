<h3>Defence Collective Singapore</h3>
<h4>Station-Based Activities (Sustainability)</h4>
<p>Discover sustainability through play! As Singapore strides towards becoming
a leader in sustainability initiatives, students have the opportunity to
contribute to this vision. At our Year-End Programme, students will explore
how they can shape Singapore’s sustainable future. They’ll come together
to unleash their creativity and innovation, engaging in activities that
highlight their role in building a greener tomorrow. Singapore Discovery
Centre is managed by Defence Collective Singapore.</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<p>1. Appreciate how innovation has played a key role in shaping Singapore.</p>
<p>2. Inspire students on how they can contribute to a future-ready Singapore.</p>
<p>3. Encourage students to adopt more environmentally conscious choices
in the way they live, work and play.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience:</strong> Pri 3 to 6 students, Sec 1 to 4/5 students,
Post-Sec (JC/Poly/ITE)</p>
<p><strong>Runtime: </strong>Oct, Nov</p>
<p><strong>Fee: </strong>Please email for more details</p>
<p><strong>Modality:</strong> In-person</p>
<p><strong>Duration of Session: </strong>3 hours (Inclusive of break)</p>
<p><strong>Programme Capacity:</strong> Min. 25 pax (per class) *Cohort visits
are highly recommended</p>
<p><strong>Mode of Registration:</strong> By email</p>
<p><strong>Due Date for Registration:</strong> 2 weeks in advance</p>
