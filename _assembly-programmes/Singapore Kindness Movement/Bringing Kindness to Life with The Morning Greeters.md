<p>Start your school day with smiles, connection, and kindness! This is a
heartwarming and interactive assembly programme that brings to life the
simple yet powerful act of greeting others with warmth and positivity.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>The Morning Greeters are a group of runners in Singapore who jog through
parks every Sunday to spread kindness by greeting strangers with cheerful
“good mornings”.</p>
<p><strong>Learning Objectives</strong>
</p>
<p>Through engaging storytelling and role-play by The Morning Greeters themselves,
students will explore:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>How a cheerful “Good morning!” can brighten someone’s day</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The power of eye contact, smiles, and kind gestures</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ways they can become Morning Greeters in their own school</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>How small actions can grow a big culture of kindness</p>
</li>
</ol>
