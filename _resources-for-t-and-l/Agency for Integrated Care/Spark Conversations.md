<p>This resource is intended for primary, secondary, and tertiary students
to engage in meaningful conversations with their loved ones through guided
questions. By sparking conversations and recording their life story and
memoirs, students will better understand their loved ones' life journey
and preferences.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>This resource will be read by students.</p>
<p><strong>Learning Objectives</strong>
</p>
<p>Students will find out more about their loved ones' life journey and preferences
through meaningful intergenerational conversations.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience</strong>: Primary 4- P6 students, Secondary 1-
4/5 students, Post Secondary (JC/ Poly/ ITE), Educators</p>
<p><strong>Runtime</strong>: All year round</p>
<p><strong>Fee</strong>: Complimentary</p>
<p><strong>Modality</strong>: Virtual</p>

</p>

</p>
