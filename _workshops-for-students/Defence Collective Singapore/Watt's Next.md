<h3>Defence Collective Singapore</h3>
<h4>Watt's Next</h4>
<p>Join us as we investigate how Singapore is going green with renewable
energy! Step up as active citizens and design solutions to resolve this
current global issue.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>• Go on an eco-trail of SDC’s very own green initiatives – Solar Panels</p>
<p>• Investigate how some renewable energy sources work</p>
<p>• Understand better how Singapore employs renewable energy and its impact
on us</p>
<p>• Design a possible solution to address an energy concern of today</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<p>1. Understand the efforts Singapore is putting in, to go green and employ
renewable energy.</p>
<p>2. Encourage a culture of sustainability and be empowered to take personal
responsibility as active citizens.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience:</strong> Sec 1 to 4/5 students, Post-Sec (JC/Poly/ITE)</p>

<p><strong>Programme Capacity:</strong> Min. 25 pax (per class); Max. 40 pax
(per class)</p>
