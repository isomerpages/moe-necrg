<h3>Defence Collective Singapore</h3>
<h4>Mission Ready</h4>
<p>Students will discover more about Military Defence, one of the pillars
of Total Defence. Through engaging and varied activities, students will
understand the importance of the army and affirm their commitment to defend
Singapore. Singapore Discovery Centre is managed by Defence Collective
Singapore.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p><u>FIELD-CRAFT EXPERIENCE [1 HOUR] </u>
</p>
<p>Students will engage in a variety of hands-on field craft activities such
as basha pitching and camouflage. They will also learn to appreciate the
role of the soldiers during jungle training.</p>
<p><u>EXPLORATION OF PERMANENT EXHIBITS GALLERY [30 MINS] </u>
</p>
<p>Students will explore the Permanent Exhibits Gallery to learn more about
past events which highlight the importance of National Service and a strong
defence force.</p>
<p><u>SAFTI MI BUS TOUR [30 MINS] </u>
</p>
<p>Hop onto our electric bus for an exclusive tour of SAFTI Military Institute
around the 88-hectare training ground for officers-to-be (closed facility,
only open for special tours). This tour offers a glimpse into the life
of an officer cadet and the importance of National Service.</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<p>1. Spark interest in soldiering and affirm studentsâ€™ commitment to Total
Defence.</p>
<p>2. Understand how National Service contributes to the peace and stability
of Singapore</p>
