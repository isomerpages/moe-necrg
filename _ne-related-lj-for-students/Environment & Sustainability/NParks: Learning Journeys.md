<p>Looking to bring your students out for a meaningful learning journey at
our parks and gardens? With the variety of different workshops and guided
walks that we organise in the various parks and gardens, we are sure that
you will find something that suits your studentsâ€™ learning needs.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>At the workshops, an instructor will engage students on various skills,
such as art or gardening. At the guided walks, a trained guide will bring
students on a tour of the park/garden.</p>
<p><strong>Learning Objectives</strong>
</p>
<p>Learn more about nature and wildlife at a workshop or learning journey
at our parks and gardens.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience</strong>: Primary 1 - 6 students, Secondary 1
- 3 students</p>
<p><strong>Runtime:</strong>&nbsp;All year round</p>
<p><strong>Fee:</strong>&nbsp;Please email for more details</p>
<p><strong>Modality:</strong>&nbsp;In-person</p>
