<h3>Defence Collective Singapore</h3>
<h4>NE Ambassadors: Let’s Begin</h4>
<p>Come sharpen your knowledge of being a National Education Ambassador by
taking on the role of a guide. Students will be taught the basics of being
a guide and will be given the opportunity to devise a spiel and guide their
peers on their chosen exhibit. Singapore Discovery Centre is managed by
Defence Collective Singapore.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>Schools will be given a choice of the following:</p>
<p>1. Singapore Discovery Centre Docent (guiding of the Permanent Exhibits
Gallery at SDC)</p>
<p>2. School Docent (guiding of the exhibits in their school’s gallery)</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<p>1. Continue their appreciation of the Singapore Story and be inspired
to guide others.</p>
<p>2. Build guiding skills and practice being an effective guide of the Singapore
Story.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience:</strong> Pri 1 - 6 students</p>
<p><strong>Runtime: </strong>All year round</p>
<p><strong>Fee: </strong>Please email for more details</p>
<p><strong>Modality:</strong> In-person</p>
<p><strong>Duration of Session: </strong>3 hours (inclusive of break)</p>
<p><strong>Programme Capacity:</strong> Min. 25 Pax (per class); Max. 40 Pax
(per class)</p>
<p><strong>Mode of Registration:</strong> By email</p>
<p><strong>Due Date for Registration:</strong> 2 weeks in advance</p>
<p><strong>Contact Info:</strong>  <a href="mailto:education@defencecollectivesg.com" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">education@defencecollectivesg.com</a>
</p>
