<p>Singapore has celebrated SG60 and Total Defence has stood strong for over
40 years. We have weathered major challenges as a nation — but the threats
we face today are often the ones that slip into everyday life. Total Defence
26 Programme is a mass, situation-based Total Defence Day programme designed
to show students that TD is not only about responding to national crises,
but it also lives in our daily actions. In each scenario, students must
think fast, support one another, and put Total Defence into action — not
in theory, but in real, relatable challenges.</p>
<p><strong>Details: </strong>The programme entails the following segments:
(1) Briefing; (2) Start of Activities and (3) Debriefing.</p>
<p><strong>Learning Objectives: </strong>
</p>
<p><strong><u>Primary</u></strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Understand how every Singaporean plays a part in Total Defence</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrate teamwork and readiness in times of challenge</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Show resilience and care to keep our community strong.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong><u>Secondary</u></strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Analyse how individual and collective actions contribute to Total Defence</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrate critical thinking and teamwork in responding to complex, real-world
challenges</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reflect on the importance of resilience and unity in safeguarding Singapore</p>
</li>
</ol>
