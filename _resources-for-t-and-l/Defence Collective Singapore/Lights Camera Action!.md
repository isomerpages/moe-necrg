<h3>Defence Collective Singapore</h3>
<h4>Lights, Camera, Action!</h4>
<p>Dive deeper into contemporary issues in Singapore through the mighty lens
of locally produced short films!</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>This programme leverages high quality short films to facilitate the discussion
and exploration of contemporary issues relevant to the current national
landscape. These student-produced films are part of an annual collaboration
between Singapore Discovery Centre and local polytechnics. There are various
film titles to choose from and they can be classified into different categories
– defence and security, racial harmony, globalization, inclusivity and
familial ties. Do write in for more details about the film choices. Singapore
Discovery Centre is managed by Defence Collective Singapore.</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<p>1. Appreciate local youth productions and how they can be an effective
medium to discuss contemporary issues in Singapore.</p>
<p>2. Reflect on how one can contribute to Singapore’s future.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience:</strong> Sec 1 to 4/5 students, Post-Sec (JC/Poly/ITE)</p>
<p><strong>Runtime: </strong>All year round</p>
<p><strong>Fee: </strong>Please email for more details</p>
<p><strong>Modality:</strong> In-person / Virtual</p>
<p><strong>Duration of Session: </strong>1 hour</p>
<p><strong>Programme Capacity:</strong> Min. 1 cohort; Max. Whole school</p>
<p><strong>Mode of Registration:</strong> By email</p>
<p><strong>Due Date for Registration:</strong> 2 weeks in advance</p>
<p><strong>Contact Info:</strong>  <a href="mailto:education@defencecollectivesg.com" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">education@defencecollectivesg.com</a>
</p>
