<h3>Defence Collective Singapore</h3>
<h4>Operations Triple Threat</h4>
<p>Embark on a self-directed game-based outdoor trail and role-play as investigators
who need to decipher clues and visit historic sites to foil a bomb threat!</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>Students will embark on a self-directed game-based outdoor trail and role-play
as investigators on a mission to foil a bomb threat by deciphering clues
and visiting four to six different historic sites that are suspected of
being the possible location of the soon-to-happen bomb blast. The sites
include, but are not limited to, Padang, Central Fire Station and Cenotaph.
Using the clues obtained at each site, students will learn about Singapore’s
defence history and the complex nature of modern security threats that
demands citizens to play a more active role.</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<p>1. Appreciate the evolving nature of security threats and understand how
everyone has a part to play in defending Singapore.</p>
<p>2. Learn about Singapore’s history from a defence-centric perspective.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience:</strong> Sec 1 to 4/5 students, Post-Sec (JC/Poly/ITE)</p>
<p><strong>Runtime: </strong>All year round</p>
