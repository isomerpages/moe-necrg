<h3>Defence Collective Singapore</h3>
<h4>Future of Food</h4>
<p>How does a small country secure its food needs? Come join us as we explore
just how Singapore has planned to feed its citizens and what we can do
to contribute to our food security.</p>
<p>• Go on an eco-trail of SDC’s very own green initiatives – Urban Garden
and Aqua Organic System</p>
<p>• Get a first-hand experience of some of our local produce</p>
<p>• Understand better how Singapore is working to secure our food security</p>
<p>• Design a possible solution to contribute to SG’s 30 by 30 goal Singapore
Discovery Centre is managed by Defence Collective Singapore</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<p>1. Understand the efforts Singapore is putting in, to secure our food
needs.</p>
<p>2. Encourage a culture of sustainability and be empowered to take personal
responsibility as active citizens.</p>
