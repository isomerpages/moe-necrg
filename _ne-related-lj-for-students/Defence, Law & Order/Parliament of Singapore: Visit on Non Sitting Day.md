<h3>Parliament of Singapore: Visit on Non Sitting Day</h3>
<p>Take a walk with us through the corridors of history and find out the
origins of our Parliament. Learn about our parliamentary system of government,
roles and functions of Parliament, who's who in the House, how laws are
made and much more.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>The Learning Journey will feature screening of Parliament's corporate
video, followed by a tour of the House to see the different exhibits and
the Chamber.</p>
<p><strong>Objective:</strong> To de-mystify Parliament House by learning
about the history, the people and how law-making works in singapore.</p>
<p><strong>Audience: </strong>Sec 1 to 4/5 students, Post-Sec (JC/Poly/ITE)</p>
<p><strong>Runtime: </strong>All year round</p>
<p><strong>Fee:</strong> Complimentary</p>
<p><strong>Modality: </strong>In-person</p>
<p><strong>Duration of Session:</strong> 1.5 hours</p>
<p><strong>Programme Capacity: </strong>30 pax minimum, 60 pax maximum</p>
<p><strong>Website:</strong>  <a href="http://www.parliament.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www.parliament.gov.sg</a>
</p>
<p><strong>Mode of Registration:</strong> By email</p>
<p><strong>Due Date for Registration:</strong> 1 month in advance</p>
