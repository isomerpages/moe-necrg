<h3>Heart&amp;Soul Showcase</h3>
<p><strong>Name of Organisation: </strong>Ministry of Digital Development
and Information (MDDI) and National Library Board (NLB)</p>
<p><strong>Programme Type:</strong> Showcase</p>
<p><strong>Programme Details:</strong>
</p>
<p>The Heart&amp;Soul showcase enables Singaporeans to connect to their past,
future, hopes and dreams, and to each other through an immersive, generative
and personalised experience, powered by plans of more than 90 agencies
and organisations, as well as the aspirations of Singaporeans.&nbsp; The
90-minute experience brings National Education to life through interactive
technology, making it an ideal learning opportunity for your school community
as part of the SG60 commemoration.</p>
<p>The Heart&amp;Soul showcase presents the following educational opportunities
for students:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students will engage with interactive exhibits that demonstrate how emerging
technologies can transform sectors like healthcare, sustainability and
transportation, and urban planning in the future; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Heart&amp;Soul will also leverage technology to create unique, customised
futures for students, based on their hopes and aspirations.</p>
</li>
</ul>
