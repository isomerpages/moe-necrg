<h3>Defence Collective Singapore</h3>
<h4>Total Defence Badgework Programme</h4>
<p>Deepen your understanding and be an advocate of the 6 pillars of Total
Defence. Explore the Permanent Exhibits Gallery and get involved in the
Guardians Of The City card game; to appreciate how Singapore navigates
evolving challenges.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p><u>BRONZE BADGE</u> 
</p>
<p>• Introduction to Total Defence through exploration of Permanent Exhibits
Gallery (SDC)</p>
<p>• Facilitated GOTC card game experience</p>
<p><u>SILVER BADGE </u>
</p>
<p>• Discuss the challenges Singapore faces and our possible responses</p>
<p>• Begin discussions about your NEMation submission</p>
<p>• Design a lesson to teach your peers how to play GOTC</p>
<p><u>GOLD BADGE</u> 
</p>
<p>• Recap of Total Defence pillars and their application in Singapore today</p>
<p>• Facilitated session to guide students on designing a possible VIA project</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<p>1. Deepen understanding of Total Defence by highlighting its increasing
relevance for a progressively complex future.</p>
<p>2. Promote active citizenry through the Appreciation, Application and
Advocacy (3As) model</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience:</strong> Sec 1 to 4/5 students</p>
<p><strong>Runtime: </strong>All year round</p>
<p><strong>Fee: </strong>Please email for more details</p>
<p><strong>Modality:</strong> In-person</p>
<p><strong>Duration of Session: </strong>3 hours (inclusive of break)</p>
<p><strong>Programme Capacity:</strong> Min. 4 class (per session); Max. 8
classes (per session)</p>
<p><strong>Mode of Registration:</strong> By email</p>
<p><strong>Due Date for Registration:</strong> 2 weeks in advance</p>
<p><strong>Contact Info:</strong>  <a href="mailto:education@defencecollectivesg.com" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">education@defencecollectivesg.com</a>
</p>
