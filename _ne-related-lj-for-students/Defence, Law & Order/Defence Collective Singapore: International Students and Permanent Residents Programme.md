<p>This programme helps students understand Singapore’s multicultural society
through fun, hands-on activities. They will experience local customs in
an interactive way that builds empathy and appreciation for the different
cultures around them. Through their visit to SDC’s Permanent Exhibit Gallery,
students will also develop a stronger sense of belonging by discovering
the shared values that unite us as Singaporeans.</p>
<p><strong>Details: </strong>
</p>
<p>This programme entails the following segments: Introduction - 10 mins,
Multiculturalism In Food - 1 hr, Multiculturalism in Games - 1 hr, Conclusion
and Key Takeaway - 35 mins</p>
