<h3>Defence Collective Singapore</h3>
<h4>NE Ambassadors: Let’s Begin</h4>
<p>Come learn more about our Singapore Story and why it is so important for
us to be able to share this story with others. Appreciate the journey Singapore
has taken with our guided tour of the Permanent Exhibits Gallery and level
up your public speaking skills in this engaging programme. Singapore Discovery
Centre is managed by Defence Collective Singapore.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p><u>INTRODUCTION [15 MINS] </u>
</p>
<p>Understand and appreciate the importance and relevance of the Singapore
Story and what it takes to be an NE Ambassador.</p>
<p><u>GUIDED TOUR @PERMANENT EXHIBITS GALLERY (SDC) [APPROX. 1.5 HOURS] </u>
</p>
<p>Understand how NE is put into practice through a guided tour of the Permanent
Exhibits Gallery.</p>
<p><u>PUBLIC SPEAKING WORKSHOP [45 MINS] </u>
</p>
<p>Understand what skills are needed to be an effective public speaker and
effective communicator. Our facilitators will provide students with both
skills and positive and negative examples, to hone their public speaking
skills.</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<p>1. Appreciate the importance of the Singapore Story and the challenges
we have experienced to build the Singapore of today.</p>
<p>2. Build public speaking skills in order to elevate the self-confidence
needed to be an ambassador of the Singapore Story.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience:</strong> Pri 1 - 6 students</p>
<p><strong>Runtime: </strong>All year round</p>
<p><strong>Fee: </strong>Please email for more details</p>
<p><strong>Modality:</strong> In-person</p>
<p><strong>Duration of Session: </strong>3 hours (inclusive of break)</p>
<p><strong>Programme Capacity:</strong> Min. 25 Pax (per class); Max. 40 Pax
(per class)</p>
<p><strong>Mode of Registration:</strong> By email</p>
