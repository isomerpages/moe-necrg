<h3>Parliament in Session</h3>
<p>Attend and observe parliamentary debates "live". Listen to Ministers and
MPs discuss and debate government policies, programmes, Bills and affairs
of state. An explanation will be provided on the proceedings in progress.
For Lower and Upper Secondary, ITE, Polytechnic and Pre-University students.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>Students will be allowed to view parliamentary proceedings from the public
galleries.</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong> To de-mystify Parliament House by learning
about the history, the people and how law-making works in singapore.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience:</strong> Sec 3 to 4/5 students, Post-Sec (JC/Poly/ITE)</p>
<p><strong>Runtime:</strong> January, February, March, April, May, July, August,
September, October, November</p>
<p><strong>Fee: </strong>Complimentary</p>
<p><strong>Modality:</strong> In-person</p>
<p><strong>Duration of Session:</strong> 1.5 hours</p>
<p><strong>Programme Capacity: </strong>10 pax minimum 25 pax maximum. Email
for more details.</p>
<p><strong>Website:</strong>  <a href="http://www.parliament.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www.parliament.gov.sg</a>
</p>
<p><strong>Mode of Registration:</strong> By email or through website link</p>
<p><strong>Due Date for Registration:</strong> Details will be given upon
receipt of email.</p>
<p><strong>Contact Info:</strong>  <a href="mailto:parl_edu@parl.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">parl_edu@parl.gov.sg</a>; 6332 5543</p>
