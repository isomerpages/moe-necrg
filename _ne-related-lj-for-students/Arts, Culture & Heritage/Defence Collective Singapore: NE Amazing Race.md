<h3>Defence Collective Singapore</h3>
<h4>N.E.MAZING RACE</h4>
<p>Put your teamwork to the test in this highly engaging race! Students will
be challenged on their ability to communicate effectively and work together
to complete a series of team challenges and emerge as champions.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>Working in groups, students are to locate challenge stations within Singapore
Discovery Centre and complete a variety of team-based activities. The team
that completes the race with the highest score and best timing wins. The
challenges were inspired by the hard work of our forefathers and the people
who helped to build Singapore, such as, the samsui women and coolies.</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<p>1. Learn the importance of teamwork and effective communication in achieving
a common goal.</p>
<p>2. Strengthen the rapport and camaraderie among peers.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience:</strong> Sec 1 to 4/5 students, Post-Sec (JC/Poly/ITE)</p>
<p><strong>Runtime: </strong>All year round</p>
<p><strong>Fee: </strong>Please email for more details</p>
<p><strong>Modality:</strong> In-person</p>
<p><strong>Duration of Session: </strong>3 hours (exclusive of break)</p>
<p><strong>Programme Capacity:</strong> Min. 100 pax (per session); Max. 300
pax (per session)</p>
<p><strong>Mode of Registration:</strong> By email</p>
<p><strong>Due Date for Registration:</strong> 2 weeks in advance</p>

</p>
