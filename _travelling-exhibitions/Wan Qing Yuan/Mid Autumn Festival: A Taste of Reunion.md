<h3>Wan Qing Yuan – Sun Yat Sen Nanyang Memorial Hall</h3>
<h4>“Mid-Autumn Festival: A Taste of Reunion” Travelling (Roving) Exhibition</h4>
<p>This travelling(roving) exhibitions are curated by Wan Qing Yuan - Sun
Yat Sen Nanyang Memorial Hall. Students can learn various aspects of Singapore’s
history and values through this exhibition.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>Falling on the 15th day of the eighth lunar month, the Mid-Autumn Festival
(also known as Mooncake Festival) is one of the most important festivals
for Chinese communities around the world as they gather in reunion during
the festival. This exhibition highlights the spirit of the Mid-Autumn Festival
and its cultural significance on family reunion and kinship love. The exhibition
is available in both English and Mandarin.</p>
<p><strong>Learning Objective:</strong> Find out more about the mooncake moulds
and artisanal mooncake makers of Singapore in the exhibition</p>
