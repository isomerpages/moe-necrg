<h3>Changi Airport Singapore</h3>
<h4>Teambonding Quest around Jewel / Changi Experience Studio</h4>
<p>Embark on a fun Amazing Jewel Changi Airport Quest and bond as a team
while discovering about the fun facts which you never knew! In teams, race
each other on a self-discovery trail across Jewel and Changi Experience
Studio to uncover clues and conquer challenges.</p>
<p>For instance, did you know that we have our own Changi Scent and Changi
Bougainvillea? Why is the shape of Jewel a toroid and not a dome, and have
you ever wondered why can't you hear the roaring sound of the plane when
you are in Jewel despite the proximity? Along the quest, discover unique
local shops and capture memorable moments with your teammates!</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>The quest will be conducted via Whatsapp. Participants need to form groups
(of 4-6 in a group) to solve a trail of clues on foot. Clues will be delivered
to all team members' phones over WhatsApp. Programme also includes admission
to Changi Experience Studio.</p>
<p><strong>Learning Objectives</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Discover fun facts about the airport and learn about major events which
took place in the airport.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Learn about our Singapore Airhub and their connectivity to other parts
of the world, as well as understand about the airport community and the
diversity of the roles which make up the community.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Learn about what makes up the design structure of Jewel and how we incorporate
sustainability efforts when designing the building</p>
</li>
</ul>
