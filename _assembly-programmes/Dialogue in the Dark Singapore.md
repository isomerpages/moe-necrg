### See My World

This educational and inspirational talk seeks to broaden studentsâ€™ perspectives of the visually impaired. By hearing the sharing of the life experiences of the blind speaker, students are encouraged to see the blind as independent individuals. In hearing how the blind speaker overcame his/her challenges, students are inspired to overcome their own obstacles.

**Details**

The programme is a 30-minute or 60-minute talk by a visually-impaired speaker. 

**Learning Objectives**

Students will be 
* Educated on the daily challenges faced by the visually impaired and be more appreciative of their abilities 
* More mindful of how to engage with a blind person 
* Inspired to overcome obstacles in their lives
