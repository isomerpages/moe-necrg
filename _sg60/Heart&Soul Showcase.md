<h3>Heart&amp;Soul Showcase (An SG60 Flagship Event)</h3>
<p><strong>Name of Organisation: </strong>Ministry of Digital Development
and Information (MDDI) and National Library Board (NLB)</p>
<p><strong>Programme Type:</strong> Exhibition</p>
<p><strong>Programme Details:</strong>
</p>
<p>Heart&amp;Soul Showcase, an SG60 flagship event, is about connecting Singaporeans
to our past, future, hopes, dreams, and more than anything else, to each
other. An immersive, multi-sensorial experience situated physically at
the heart of town, to inspire visitors with a sense of optimism for their
future and the future of Singapore. Smart Nation will continue to power
our aspirations and better the way we live, work, play, learn and care
– where people, businesses and the nation thrive with technology.</p>
<p>An exciting NE learning journey awaits students at the showcase:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Personalised Digital Experience: Each student creates their own unique
avatar and receives a smart device with an audio guide, bringing them through
a journey of discovery at the showcase.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Future-Ready Installations: Through cutting-edge technology, students
engage with interactive displays that respond to their presence and choices,
creating a uniquely personal experience.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>‘Through the Generations’ Storytelling: Students discover Singapore's
transformation through perspectives across different generations, fostering
understanding of our heritage and our collective vision.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Inspiration &amp; Reflection: The showcase aims to inspire students on
the limitless possibilities for Singapore’s future and encourages students
to reflect on how their beliefs and potential actions will shape our nation's
development.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborative Vision: In the finale, students contribute their aspirations
for Singapore's future, joining a collective showcase of hopes and dreams
from visitors across all walks of life.The Heart&amp;Soul showcase enables
Singaporeans to connect to their past, future, hopes and dreams, and to
each other through an immersive, generative and personalised experience,
powered by plans of more than 90 agencies and organisations, as well as
the aspirations of Singaporeans.&nbsp; The 90-minute experience brings
National Education to life through interactive technology, making it an
ideal learning opportunity for your school community as part of the SG60
commemoration.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Programme Runtime:</strong> 27 August to 31 December 2025 (Schools
are invited to visit during the soft launch in early Aug)</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience: </strong>Students in Primary schools, Secondary
schools &amp; Pre-Us</p>
<p><strong>Cost of Admission: </strong>Free</p>
<p><strong>Contact Info: </strong>For any queries, please email <a href="mailto:ivan_tay@mddi.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">ivan_tay@mddi.gov.sg</a>
</p>
<p><strong>Link: </strong>To book a visit for your school, please submit
your booking via <a href="https://go.gov.sg/heartandsoul-groupbooking" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/heartandsoul-groupbooking</a>
</p>
<p><em>Please note that advanced booking is required for school groups.&nbsp; Schools will need to cater your own transport for this event.</em>
</p>
