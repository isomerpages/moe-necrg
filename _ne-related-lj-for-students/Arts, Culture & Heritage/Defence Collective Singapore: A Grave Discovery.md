<h3>Defence Collective Singapore</h3>
<h4>A Grave Discovery</h4>
<p>Delve deeper into Singapore’s past through an exciting journey exploring
historic sites in and around Fort Canning!</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>Students will embark on a self-guided game-based outdoor trail which allows
them to explore the significant historic sites and landmarks in and around
Fort Canning. This trail will take them on a thrilling journey that requires
them to put on their creative thinking caps to solve puzzles or complete
challenges to navigate from one station to the next.</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<p>1. Understand and appreciate Singapore’s past through the exploration
of significant historic sites and landmarks.</p>
<p>2. Appreciate Singapore’s journey in becoming the successful and independent
country that she is today.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience:</strong> Sec 1 to 4/5 students, Post-Sec (JC/Poly/ITE)</p>
<p><strong>Runtime: </strong>All year round</p>
<p><strong>Fee: </strong>Please email for more details</p>
<p><strong>Modality:</strong> In-person</p>
<p><strong>Duration of Session: </strong>4 hours (inclusive of travelling
time)</p>
<p><strong>Programme Capacity:</strong> Min. 35 pax (per session); Max. 40
pax (per session)</p>
<p><strong>Mode of Registration:</strong> By email</p>
<p><strong>Due Date for Registration:</strong> 2 weeks in advance</p>
