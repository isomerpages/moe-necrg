<h3>Wan Qing Yuan – Sun Yat Sen Nanyang Memorial Hall</h3>
<h4>“Celebrating Commonalities: Festive Customs in Singapore” Travelling (Roving) Exhibition</h4>
<p>The travelling (roving) exhibition is co-curated by Wan Qing Yuan - Sun
Yat Sen Nanyang Memorial Hall and River Valley High School. Students can
learn various aspects of Singapore’s history and values through this exhibition.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>Singapore takes pride in its multi-ethnic and multi-religious society.
It is home to people from diverse ethnic and religious backgrounds, such
as the Chinese, Malays, Indians and Eurasians. How do these different communities
prepare for and celebrate their festivals? What are the similarities among
them? Co-curated by Sun Yat Sen Nanyang Memorial Hall and students from
River Valley High School, “Celebrating Commonalities: Festive Customs in
Singapore” highlights various aspects in the preparation and celebration
of different festivals, offering insights into the colourful cultural fabric
that makes Singapore so unique. The exhibition is available in both English
and Mandarin.</p>
<p><strong>Learning Objective: </strong>Learn the various aspects in the
preparation and celebration of different festivals, offering insights into
the colourful cultural fabric that makes Singapore so unique!</p>
