<h3>Defence Collective Singapore</h3>
<h4>Bravo Zulu</h4>
<p>Dive into the Singapore Navy Museum and learn more about our maritime
history, the roles and responsibilities of the Republic of Singapore Navy
(RSN) and the importance of safeguarding our waters. Working in groups,
students are to locate challenge stations within the Singapore Navy Museum
and complete a variety of team-based activities. Each station will have
a facilitator who will share some insights about the RSN and guide the
students into completing the Navy-related activity. Singapore Navy Museum
is managed by Defence Collective Singapore</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<p>1. To understand how busy the Singapore Strait is and why it is important
to keep it safe.</p>
<p>2. To appreciate the role of RSN in safeguarding Singapore waters.</p>
<p><strong>Target Audience:</strong> Sec 1 - 4/5 students</p>
<p><strong>Runtime: </strong>All year round</p>
