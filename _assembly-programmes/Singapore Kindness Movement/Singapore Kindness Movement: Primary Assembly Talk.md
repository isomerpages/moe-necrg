<p>Little Mighty Warriors is a fun and empowering assembly session led by
Joshua Khoo, founder of the Little Mighty Warriors movement. Born with
Achondroplasia, Joshua shares his personal journey to help students understand
that no one is too small to make a big difference.</p>
<p><strong>Details</strong>
</p>
<p>Through lively storytelling and relatable scenarios, students will be
encouraged to become everyday heroes by embracing inclusivity, empathy,
and courage.</p>
<p><strong>Learning Objectives</strong>
</p>
<p>In this inspiring session, students will learn:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>What it means to include others and help them feel like they belong</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>How to stand up for classmates who may be left out or treated unfairly</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The importance of empathy and kindness in everyday actions</p>
</li>
